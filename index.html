Profile

小説のプロット作成や執筆をAIで支援するBunChoというツールを開発中.

Born in Chiba prefecture in Japan in 1997. I graduated from Kaisei Junior & Senior High School. Since my first year at the University of Tsukuba, I have been focusing on HCI applications of Optics and Machine Learning. Presented in international conferences such as CHI2021 alt.chi, NIPS 2017 Workshop (Highlited to Oral presentation), ACE 2017 Paper, SIGGRAPH 2017 Poster. I'm developing a tool called BunCho, which is an AI-assisted plotting and writing tool for novels.
