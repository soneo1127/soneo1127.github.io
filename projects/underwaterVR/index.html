Optimized HMD System for Underwater VR Experience

We proposed HMD suitable for underwater use. We optically internally designed the HMD so that it can be seen with a wide viewing angle even if the internal structure is filled with water. We can enjoy VR underwater with less environment setting.

Hiroyuki Osone＊1, Takatoshi Yoshida＊2, Yoichi Ochiai＊1

＊1 Unibersity of Tsukuba, ＊2 The University of Tokyo

水中でVR体験をするために，ヘッドマウンテッドディスプレイ(HMD)の構造を内部が水で満たされるように設計し，水中での浮力を小さくしました．

大曽根宏幸，吉田貴寿，落合陽一
